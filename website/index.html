Aoi Kanzaki

Aoi Kanzaki is a Multi-Functional discord bot packed with many many features. Remind yourself something, or even play some music in a voice channel! Got a pesky mod that keeps changing channels and you can't track down who did it? Setup Aoi's modlog system in your server!

Message from Jonny#0181

I created Aoi a long time ago, the project first started with the name Brooklyn Bot about 7 years ago... I then moved on a and changed the name to F.res.h and worked on it with a very old friend of mine. Then just recently changed the name to Aoi. I've been working on this project for who knows how long. It's always just been a little hobby project of mine that I was doing in my freetime and to help out fellow community owners and friends.

Features

Music

Music module is packed with a lot of features. You can link your private Spotify account and access all of your playlists and liked songs inside discord.

Moderation

Moderation is packed with a few features, like warning, mod logging and more.

Utility

Utility is packed with your basic utility commands like reminders and more.

Leveling

Leveling is your basic leveling system with level roles you can setup yourself.

Welcomer

Welcome is pretty basic for now, but expect more in the future!

Starboard

Starboard is a pretty basic Starboard. But, you have the ability to set a custom required amount of reactions.
